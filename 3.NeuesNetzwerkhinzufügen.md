## PLABDM01:

<br></br>

### Firewall PfSense:

WAN => hn0 | Option 2 | IP-Address: 10.71.31.2 | 24 | Gateway: 10.71.31.254 | Standard Gateway: y

LAN => hn1 | Option 2 | IP-Address: 192.168.0.1 | 24 | Upstream Gateway : 10.71.31.254 | Standard Gateway: y

HR => hn2 | Option 2 | IP-Address: 192.168.2.1 | 24 | Upstream Gateway : 10.71.31.254 | Standard Gateway: y

<br></br>


### Hyper-V Einstellungen:

Hyper-V | pfSense turn off

Hyper-V | virtueller Switch Manager | Privat | Human Ressources | privat Network | Apply

pfSense Settings | Network Adapter add | virtueller Switch HR

<br></br>


### Firewall im Browser:

Firewall 10.71.31.2 im Browser (DC01) | Interfaces | OPT1 zu HR umbennen | enable Interface |

IPv4 configuration statisch | 192.168.2.1 /24 um 2 Interface zu aktivieren

<br></br>
